Each tile is a real asset streamed through a stateless AWS Lambda backed by DynamoDB (per-asset rolling state) — health index, anomaly score, and remaining-useful-life computed on every reading. When a confident downward trend crosses the lead-time horizon, an edge-triggered alert fires once and (optionally) Amazon Bedrock drafts the work order. Gradual wear-out is caught with days of lead time at zero false alarms; abrupt faults are the floor. Downtime-avoided assumes each early catch converts an unplanned failure into a planned intervention.
